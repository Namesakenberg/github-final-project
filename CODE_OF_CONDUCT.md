# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a respectful and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and considerate
* Provide constructive feedback
* Collaborate in a positive manner

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or discrimination
* Offensive or abusive language
* Personal attacks

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will take appropriate action in response to any unacceptable behavior.
